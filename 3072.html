3072

SLIDE. MERGE. REACH 3072.

The classic 2048 sliding-tile puzzle, supercharged with three powerful skills. Match tiles, chain combos, break through walls, and see if you can push past 2048 to the legendary 3072.

GET IT ON Google Play COMING SOON App Store

HOW TO PLAY

  1. Slide a row or column in any of the four directions to push every tile that way.
  2. Merge two tiles of the same value when they collide — they combine into the next tier.
  3. Chain merges in a single move for huge combo scores.
  4. Use skills when the board gets tight. Each skill charges as you play.
  5. Reach 3072 — past the classic 2048 ceiling, into the next level of mastery.

THREE SKILLS TO BREAK THROUGH

Stuck on a wall of high tiles? Use one of three mid-board skills to turn the tide. Each one is limited per game — use them wisely.

💣

BOMB

Detonate any tile on the board. All four neighbours lose half their value — prime setup for a chain merge.

🧽

SWAP

Exchange any two tiles on the board. Rescue a cornered 1024 or set up a perfect four-way merge.

✨

GENERATE

Drop a fresh tile of your choice onto any empty cell. The board becomes yours again.
